Guidelines for Submission

Festival Date: July 6, 2013

Deadline for Submission: May 10, 2013

We are looking for 15-20 minute films.Project must fit GHFF's categories. We currently support films in the following categories with some examples:

Maternal Health

Child health/injuries (e.g congenital defects)

Injuries

Other essestial surgical conditions

Emergency Care

Projects must explore a discrete issue in any of the categories mentioned. We are particularly interested in projects that also display:

Creative and innovative storytelling

Ongoing social issue

Potential for engagement

We do not accept films advertising a charity, NGO, NPO, or any commercial product, whether implicit or explicit in the film. Submitted films should either be in English or include English subtitles. If a film is selected for theater presentation at the festival it is the responsibility of the filmmaker to provide GHFF with either an HDCAM tape or a 35mm film exhibition copy by June 15, 2013.
