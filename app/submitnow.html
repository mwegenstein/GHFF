Submission Instructions

Submissions will be open through May 10, 2013. No films will be accepted after May 10, 2013. All submissions must come through our online form here (hyperlink) or see the link below.

Be prepared to provide the following on the form:

  1. Contact Information
  2. Full film information including a short (5-7 sentences) synopsis
  3. A link to an online screener OR 2 DVDs
  4. Acceptance of Rights and Releases

Submission Materials

We highly encourage you to submit the film and ALL materials online. We will not download film files: all online entries must be hosted by the filmmaker or a third party (ie, Vimeo.com). If online entry is not possible, entrants must submit 2 DVDs (any region, NTSC encouraged). Please DO NOT include ANY promotional materials in your submission.

Shipping Information

Mailing address below. If you chose to submit your film by mail you are responsible for all shipping charges to IGOT GHFF and for any customs clearances and/or associated fees and taxes. Entry materials will not be returned.
