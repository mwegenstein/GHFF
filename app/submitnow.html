Submission instructions

The submission season for 2013 will be open through May 10, 2013. No films will be accepted outside this time period. All submissions must come through our online form (link below).

Before clicking on the link to fill out the submission form, please be prepared to provide the following:

  1. Contact Information
  2. Full film information including a short synopsis
  3. A link to an online screener OR 2 DVDs
  4. Acceptance of Rights and Releases

Submit Now - hyperlink to form

Submission Materials

We highly encourage you to submit ALL materials online, including the film itself. However, we will not download film files: all online entries must be hosted by the filmmaker or a third party (ie, Vimeo.com). If online entry is not possible, entrants must submit 2 DVDs (any region, NTSC encouraged). Please DO NOT include ANY promotional materials in your submission.

Shipping Information

If you chose to submit your film by mail you are responsible for all shipping charges to IGOT GHFF and for any customs clearances and/or associated fees and taxes. Entry materials will not be returned.
