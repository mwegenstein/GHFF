Our Partners

Institute for Global Orthopedics and Traumatology (IGOT)

The Institute for Global Orthopedics and Traumatology is a non-profit organization dedicated to improve the care of underserved populations affected by orthopaedic trauma injuries worldwide. IGOT's work is rooted in a commitment to long-term sustainability and impact and spans efforts to improve education, research, and advocacy for surgical care in developing countries.

International Collaboration for Essential Surgery (ICES)

The International Collaboration for Essential Surgery is a group of experts in the field of international surgical care dedicated to promoting the effective provision of essential low-cost surgical care to save lives and prevent serious disability in low-resource settings.

Own2Feet

Own2Feet is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the quality of life for diabetic patients and amputees by developing affordable, sustainable solutions. Own2Feet is committed to empowering local communities by teaching skills in prosthetic fabrication and management while promoting diabetic awareness and amputation prevention.

ALittle4Alot

ALittle4Alot is a non-profit organization which aims to help women who have undergone surgical repair of obstetric fistulas return to school or receive vocational skills training.
